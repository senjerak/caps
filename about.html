Cap's is a silly, interactive website featuring a sample of my little Mushroom Cats!

This is my first website ever. It was love at first sight! Love at first... thought? I had initially come up with the idea
for Cap's back in 2019 when I was thinking of new plot projects for animation school. Mushrooms have always been really cute
and interesting to me and I have always enjoyed making art that helps my friends and I smile. I started Web Development
in 2022 and couldn't be more excited to continue learning. I hope that you enjoy my website and all the hard work I put into it!

When you click on an emerging Cap, they'll crawl out to follow your cursor around on screen!
