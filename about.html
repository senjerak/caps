When you click on an emerging Cap... they'll come crawl out to follow your cursor around on screen!

Cap's is a silly, interactive website featuring a sample of my little Mushroom Cats!

This is my first website ever. It was love at first 'site'...! I had initially come up with the idea
for Cap's back in 2019 when I was thinking of new plot projects for animation school. Mushrooms have
always been really cute and interesting to me.. And I have always enjoyed making art that helps
me and my friends smile..! I started Web Development in 2022 and couldn't be more
excited to continue learning. I hope that you enjoy my website and all the hard work I put into it!
